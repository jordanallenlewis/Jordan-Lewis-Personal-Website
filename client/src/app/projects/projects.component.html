  • Redesigned LLNL.gov
  • Website Mockup
  • Written Report PDF
  • I used Axure to create a mockup redesign of the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory website.
  • The goal of this project was to make measurable improvement on the design of LLNL.gov (i.e. shorter time between the user loading the website and finding the desired information).
  • After running an independent sample two-tailed T-test on the total task times of two groups of participants in the study (original website vs the redesigned website) with an alpha value of 0.05, I concluded that the difference in time to find the specified information was significant with a t-value of 2.64575 and a p-value of .029449.
